Using our Chatbot, we help parents, educator and people with Down Syndrome to solve their questions, get resources and support. Our objective is to improve life quality and increase awareness about Down Syndrome.

How we can help

For Parents

We are a community of parents that support children and youth with Down Syndrome. We work together to improve life quality and access to information of our loved ones.

For Educators

Teaching children and tenagers with special needs requires to be constatly updated. We support you by offering up-to-date resources to facilitate your work.

For Children

We provide interactive materials and activities to help your learning.
